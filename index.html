Serving Those Who Served

At Leaf Home, we proudly support our veterans and military families. We believe that service deserves opportunity — and we’re honored to create meaningful careers for those who have sacrificed for our country.

Dedicated to Veterans & Military Spouses

Our Head of Military is recognized as a Military Friendly® Veteran Champion of the Year in Corporate America. As a company, Leaf Home has received the DAV Patriot Employer Award, the MOAA Impact Award, and Congressional Special Recognition for our dedication to the veteran community.

If you're exploring opportunities through SkillBridge, Hiring Our Heroes, or the Army P3 Program, our Military Engagement Team is here to support your journey.
